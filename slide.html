Semver

  1. major: Would your change require changes to somebody's application code? If so it's a major version bump. These could include a breaking change to a component's appearance or behaviour, a major visual change to an image asset, or a big deprecation.
  2. feature: A new component, image asset, or service added
  3. patch: A bug fix. A non-breaking change to a service or component API. A refactor that does not affect the appearance or behaviour of a component.
  4. nochange: that isn't ready for use yet or doesn't need to be published to npm (e.g. documentation).
